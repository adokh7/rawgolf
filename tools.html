03The Small Print

How these actually work

Nothing leaves your phone

Every calculation happens in JavaScript on your own device. There is no server, no database and no account. Where a tool remembers your group or your last round, it uses your browser's local storage, which stays on your device and which you can wipe from inside each tool. We cannot see your scores, your bets or your handicap — and we would rather not know.
