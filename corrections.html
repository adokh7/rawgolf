DOCUMENT P-04 THE CORRECTIONS LOG POLICY NOTHING DELETED, EVER

Every mistake. In public. Forever.

Every publication makes mistakes. Most bury them. This page is the opposite: every error we've published, what we got wrong, when we fixed it, and who caught it — newest first, preserved permanently. We can't promise we'll never be wrong. We can promise you'll never have to wonder.

How this ledger works Errors are logged within 24 hours of confirmation. The original mistake stays visible, struck through — memory-holing is the coward's edit (Tenet T3). Severity is graded: FACTUAL (wrong stat, wrong quote, wrong claim) or MINOR (typos, broken links, mislabeled dates). Whoever catches it gets named, unless they'd rather not be. Catch one yourself: corrections@rawgolf.com — best catch of the month gets your name on the fix.
ERR-002 LOGGED 11 JUN 2026 · FIXED SAME DAY FACTUAL
"Frame by Frame: The Move That Fixed Rahm's Start Lines"
We wrote "...a grip change worth 14 yards of dispersion improvement."
The truth The correct figure from the shot data is 11.6 yards. We rounded from memory instead of from the spreadsheet. The article body now shows the correct number with this correction appended.
Caught by @FairwayFinder_NL, reader, who pulled the same dataset and did the math properly. Respect.
ERR-001 LOGGED 04 JUN 2026 · FIXED IN 3 HOURS MINOR
"The Quiet Death of the Monday Qualifier Dream"
We wrote Dateline read "TUE 03 JUN" on a story published Wednesday the 4th.
The truth Published WED 04 JUN. A template artifact from the draft. Boring mistake, logged anyway — the ledger doesn't get to pick and choose.
Caught by R. Duarte, the story's own author, ten minutes after publish. Self-reported errors count double around here.
